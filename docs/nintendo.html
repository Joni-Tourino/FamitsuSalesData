Nintendo dominated the charts both on consoles and games

by Jonathan Touriño Jacobo

The console reached 15 million units sold

Since its launch in 2017, the Nintendo Switch keeps on selling, outperforming its numbers year after year. Lifetime Switch is at 15 million.


Some stats for the Nintendo Switch in 2020:

  • The release of the Lite version keeps boosting sales of the Nintendo Switch who passed 5,5 M in its last month of the year. The base line has steadily sold a similar amount every year: 3,4 million units.
  • In November the Switch already passed the total from 2019, and if it keeps the pace of 200,000 consoles sold, for the remaining three weeks, it'll end the year with more than 6 million consoles sold in Japan. Despite having stock issues during February and at the beginning of March.
  • Nintendo released 9 games on its hybrid console for 2020, with Animal Crossing: New Horizons being its biggest success in years. The launch week of Animal Crossing allowed for the console to have its best week ever, with more than 390,000 units sold between the two models. Better even than the launch week of the Nintendo Switch, with 330,000.
  • The lack of competition in the market, mainly due to a scarcity of stock for the PlayStation 5 around the world, and strong sales from Animal Crossing puts, currently, the console as the 9th most sold in Japan. And with 90,000 units of difference with the Super Nintendo, it'll end 2020 in 8th position.
  • Counting both the Nintendo Switch and Lite models, 2020 will make it the third best year for a console, behind the Nintendo DS' performances in 2006 and 2007, according to the journalist Oscar Lemaire.

Only Switch games in the top 30?

In the second week of December, out of the 30 best retail games, 28 were for the Nintendo Switch, while the remaining two for the PlayStation 4. A first, and not the last, in the past 20 years of Famitsu's charts.

That week, Cyberpunk 2077 was the only game not released for the Switch. With the last two weeks of December to be counted, we may end up having a list were all the games are for a single console for the first time in more than 20 years.

Older Nintendo games keep on selling

Games published in 2017 or 2018 showed to have good legs in 2020. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe and Splatoon 2 were able to outperform 2019 sale numbers. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, which was released back in 2017 with the Nintendo Switch, only had a 20 % decrease in sales compared to 2019.
Of all the big names from Nintendo, Super Mario Odyssey dipped the most with a decline of 70 percent, despite being the 35th anniversary since the release of Super Mario Bros. Depending on how well Super Mario Party sells during the last two weeks of the year, it may end up having even numbers with 2019.


A year of milestones

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate and Pokemon Sword/Shield will end up the year with 4 million games sold in retail, joining Animal Crossing: New Horizons. And if Mario Kart 8 Deluxe numbers stay similar than 2020, it may join the group by the end of 2021.
Ring Fit Adventure, is the 10th Nintendo (according to their last financial results) game to reach 2 million copies sold and it did so the week before Christmas.


Ring Fit Adventure ends the year strong


  • Ring Fit Adventure had stock issues by the end of January, not just in Japan, and the coronavirus made things more difficult. Even with those issues, the game only left once the top 10 of the week.
  • If it was the only game affected, this is due to the fact it comes with a Ring Con and a leg strap for each of the controllers. This means the game can only be sold in a physical format.
  • Fitness Boxing (published by Imagineer) benefited from that problem, and sold an additional 43,000 units despite not being in the top 30 for several months earlier. They released a second one in December, who up until now has sold less than 25,000 copies.
  • Two more weeks are still to be counted. Famitsu won't release the numbers until early next year.

Pikmin 3 Deluxe has already passed its Wii U version

The first week for Pikmin 3 Deluxe was the best one in the franchise. That's despite being an enhanced version of the Wii U version.
The total sales are already double Pikmin 3, and with less than 100,000 copies to reach Pikmin. The game has had strong sales in December, with 40,000 in the week prior to Christmas. The game will either become the best selling, without taking into account digital sales, in the franchise by the end of the year or at the beginning of 2021.
