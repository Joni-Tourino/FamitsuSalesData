Nintendo Switch kept sales of consoles steady

by Jonathan Touriño Jacobo

Lifetime for the Nintendo Switch is at 17 million in Japan

For a fourth year in a row, it's the best selling console and keeps beating its previous year

The Nintendo Switch was mainly the only console selling this year, with almost nine out of ten of total sales. Even counting only Nintendo's consoles numbers, it passes by a few thousands the total of 2019.
Sony and Microsoft launched their next generation of consoles in November; The PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series S|X, respectively. First week for the PlayStation 5 was shy of 120,000, while the Xbox Series totaled 20,000 consoles sold. The transition to a new generation, and the lack of stock for the PlayStation 5, leaves Sony under the million units sold between PlayStation 4 and 5's totals.
