Highlights of the week:

  • Strangely enough, we had to wait until the beginning of March to get the first new release for the PS 5 with Sega's Yakuza Like a Dragon. As a comparison, the PS 4 version sold more than 150,000 units in its first week, a year ago.
  • Momotaro Dentetsu: Showa, Heisei, Reiwa mo Teiban! became the first non-Nintendo game to pass the 2 million retail games (only) for the Nintendo Switch.
  • Next week will have a few milestones with Nintendo and Pokémon games.
