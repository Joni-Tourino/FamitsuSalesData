Highlights of the week:

  • A Monster Hunter game makes a comeback, which could be connected with the demo of the upcoming Monster Hunter Rise (for the Switch) that was made available on 7 January.
  • Momotaro Dentetsu is the only title that saw an increase (of 1 in 4 units) from last week. The game has sold over 100,000 units for eight of the nine weeks it has been on the market. Animal Crossing managed to have 14 weeks (11 in a row) over the six figure numbers in 2020.
  • On the console side, the Nintendo Switch has been selling over 100,000 machines for 11 weeks in a row. The last seven with more than 150,000.
