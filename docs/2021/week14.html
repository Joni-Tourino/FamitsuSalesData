Highlights of the week:

  • This week 9 of 10 games in the first places were million sellers, with only Super Mario 3D World + Bowser's Fury below that number.
  • Ring Fit Adventure passed the 2.5 million mark this week, showing that the game keeps on selling quite well. This is the 6th Nintendo Switch game to pass that threshold. Five of them are published by Nintendo while the other one is from the Pokemon Company.
  • Outriders managed to stay on the list and avoided a complete top 30 with only Nintendo Switch games.
  • The 3D Anniversary compilation of Mario games left the list, but Super Mario Odyssey took its place; after a month out of the top 30.
