Highlights of the week:

  • In its second week, Monster Hunter Rise's sales almost dropped 80%, with an extra 277,000 units sold.
  • With the end of March and Mario's death, Super Mario 3D All-Stars will slowly disappear from the top 30. With physical sales at less than 600,000 units.
