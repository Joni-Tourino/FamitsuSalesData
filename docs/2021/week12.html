Highlight of the week:

  • To no surprise, Monster Hunter Rise is the best seller of the week and opens with more than a million units. This is the third Nintendo Switch game to have a six figure launch week (Pokémon Sword/Shield and Animal Crossing being the other two), and the first third party game to do so. Still, the numbers aren't enough to beat previous games in the franchise.

Highlights of the week:

  • The Nintendo Switch is the console with the highest increase this week (200% rise) with more than 250'000 units sold. Compared with last year's big launch Animal Crossing: New Horizon it didn't manage to outperform that week. Total sales are still higher than last year, which was its best.
  • Good week for the PlayStation 5 too, with its best week yet (aside from launch week). Sony seems to have finally managed the stock issues for the Japanese market.
