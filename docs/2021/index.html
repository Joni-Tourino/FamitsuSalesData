by Jonathan Touriño Jacobo

2021 Week by Week

Each week, Famitsu posts the weekly retail sales of video games and consoles in Japan. In this page you'll be able to see the weekly sales of video games for 2021. With highlights (either written or in form of charts) of the week both for games and consoles.
Quick clarification, Week 1 here is according to the natural calendar (4 January to 10 January) and not Famitsu's own calendar (28 December to 3 January). Which is the one most sites will be following for the year ahead.
I made that choice because I based my 2020 year recap in 53 weeks (from 30 December 2019 to 3 January 2021), and to avoid counting twice that last week.
Preliminary results (top 10 and consoles) are published on Thursdays, and the rest of the top 30 the day after.


  • Week 1 (4 January - 10 January)
  • Week 2 (11 January - 17 January), top 10 and consoles numbers will be published on Thursday
