Highlights of the week:

  • A few new releases this week with the newest installment from the Story of Seasons series that sold more than 200,000 copies. This is the best launch in Marvelous' series.
  • Square Enix's Bravely Default II sold around 93,000 units. It's the lowest for the series, but quite close to Bravely Second: End Layer's launch sales (with 97,000). This is without taking into account that digital sales are more important now than they were when the other episodes released.
