Highlights of the week:

  • Week 6 saw the release of Super Mario 3D World + Bowser's Fury, with a little bit over a quarter of a million copies sold. The game sold more than double the Wii U version, as you can see in the chart below (with games from both the Wii U and the Nintendo Switch).


  • The other new release making the top 10 is Little Nightmares 2, and the game sold around 35,000 copies between the Switch and the PS4.
    • Greatly improving the numbers from the first one in 2018, as they were barely passing 12,000 units.
