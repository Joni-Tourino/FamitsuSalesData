2021: A weekly view of Famitsu sales

by Jonathan Touriño Jacobo

2021 Week by Week

Each week, Famitsu posts the weekly sales of software and hardware of the videogame industry in Japan. In this page you'll be able to navigate through each week, the sales of consoles and which games made the top 30 in the retail market.
If you want to see how 2020 was, you can access it in the top of the page.


Higlights of the week:

  • The PlayStation 4 is still putting some resistence, thanks to Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War. We will have to wait another week, at least, to see a top 30 of a single console.
  • Fitness games seem to be quite prised this week, with 3 games around that genre. Imagineer (see tweet below) announced earlier in the week that shipments and sales worldwide of Fitness Boxing 2: Rhythm & Exercise passed half a million copies. One in ten are for the Japanese retail market.
