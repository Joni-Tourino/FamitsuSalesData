2021: Week 1 - The same ones as we ended the year

Week 1 (4 January to 10 January) - Video games

Each week, Famitsu posts the weekly retail sales of software and hardware of the video game industry in Japan. In this page you'll be able to navigate through each week, the sales of consoles and which games made the top 30 in the retail market.
If you want to see how 2020 was, you can access it in the top of the page.



Highlights of the week:

  • All games suffered a big drop from the previous week, which is normal after the post holiday season.
  • The PlayStation 4 doesn't want to leave the top 30, thanks to Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War (Position 24). We will have to wait another week, at least, to see if a record top 30 of a single console will happen.
  • Fitness games seem to be quite popular this week, with 3 games around that genre. The most popular one, Ring Fit Adventure, passed Super Mario Odyssey in retail sales. Meanwhile, Imagineer (see tweet below) announced earlier in the week that shipments and sales worldwide of Fitness Boxing 2: Rhythm & Exercise passed half a million copies. One in ten of those sales are for the Japanese retail market.

  • Animal Crossing: New Horizons passed 6,5 million retail copies sold.
  • Pikmin 3 passed the retail sales numbers from Pikmin on GameCube, and currently sits at 513,225 copies. It also makes it the fastest selling game of the franchise, given that Pikmin's numbers were after more than a year.
